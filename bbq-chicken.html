Slow Cooker BBQ Chicken Sandwiches

Description

Super easy BBQ chicken sandwiches that are tasty and budget friendly. Some coleslaw, garlic corn, and a pickle go great with these sandwiches. Or to save time, a bag of chips.

ingredients

  1. 4 large bone-in chicken breast halves, skin removed, trimmed of fat
  2. 1 (18 ounce) bottle barbeque sauce
  3. 1.5 (16 ounce) packages (4-pack) whole wheat hamburger buns, split

Steps

  1. Place chicken in a slow cooker and pour barbecue sauce over top.
  2. Cover and cook on Low until chicken is tender and can easily be pulled apart with a fork.
  3. Remove chicken breasts from the slow cooker. Separate meat and bones; discard bones. Shred chicken with a fork.
  4. Place chicken on buns with some extra sauce from the slow cooker.
